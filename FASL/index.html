SLBC

FASL has already supported several athletes in securing trials with premier league clubs, including FC Kallon and East End Lions.
Community stakeholders describe the initiative as “a major boost” to youth football development in Sierra Leone.

AYV

FASL has organized showcase tournaments that attracted attention from both local coaches and international scouts. Parents and local coaches applaud the agency for offering structured
pathways and meaningful opportunities for young footballers across the country.

AMAZE TV

Amaze TV Sports highlights the growing impact of the Football Agency in Sierra Leone (FASL), calling it “one of the fastest rising platforms for football talent.”
The agency continues to support youth players with mentorship, training sessions, and exposure to high level competitions.
