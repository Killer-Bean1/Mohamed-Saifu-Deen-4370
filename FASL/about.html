ABOUT FASL

Empowering Sierra Leone's Football Future

Our Mission

To discover, develop, and promote exceptional football talent across Sierra Leone, creating pathways for young athletes to achieve their dreams both locally and internationally.

Our Vision

To become the premier football agency in West Africa, recognized for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and the holistic development of our players.

What We Do

We scout promising talent from districts nationwide, provide professional training programs, guide athletes through career decisions, and create opportunities with top clubs.

Our Impact

Having scouted and trained 150+ young players, we've successfully secured trials with premier clubs like East End Lions, FC Kallon, and Bo Rangers.

Our Values

Integrity: We operate with transparency and honesty in all our dealings.

Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in training and development.

Community: We believe in giving back and supporting football development at all levels.

Innovation: We embrace modern training methods and technology to enhance player development.
